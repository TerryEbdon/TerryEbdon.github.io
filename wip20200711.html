Gradle version

I noticed, while reviewing yesterday's notes, that I have a Gradle 6.0.1 install. Switching to that works fine. This Gradle release is from November 2019, so significantly less ancient than the version I'd rolled back to.

Change of plans

I'd intended to spend some time investigating Gradle builds of Eclipse WindowBuilder projects. The good weather changed my plans. It finally stopped raining, so I instead spent several hours digging out tree stumps, felling a small tree that was too close to the house and clearing space in front of the fuel sheds. That's going to leave me pretty stiff on Sunday. I crushed my Fitbit step goal, getting in 19,208 steps.
